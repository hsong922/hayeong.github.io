{human-computer interaction, data analytics, information visualization}

I am a first-year PhD student inComputer Science at Georgia Tech. I am interested in answering questions like how can visualization support analysis and insight in large datasets? How can visualization systems allow machines and people to work collaboratively?

I'm working with Prof. John Stasko at the Georgia Tech Visualization Lab. My current research focuses on how to deal with missing values in terms of representations for analysis. Also, I am working on combining multimodal (speech and touch) to visual interface to support data analysis.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science at the Handong Global University in 2016. I received my M.S. in Computer Science as a part of VisuaLab under Prof. Danielle Albers Szafir at the University of Colorado, Boulder in 2018.
